A Little Something About Me.

Hi, I’m Abdulqadir Abuharrus, but go by Abdo. Born and raised in Tripoli, Libya. I grew up camping and swimming along the Mediterranean coast. My dad, who used to be a tour guide, cultivated a love for traveling and all things outdoors. I’ve been incredibly fortunate to have traveled as much as I have at my age. Along the way, I’ve snagged a few photos here & there and would like to share them with you here.
